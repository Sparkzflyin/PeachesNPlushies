Frequently Asked Questions

What does Peaches & Plushies sell?

Peaches & Plushies sells hand sewn felt plushies, pins, stickers, keychains, and more. Our signature line is The Orchard Pals Collection.

Are the plushies mass-produced?

No, every plushie is 100% hand-made and meticulously stitched by hand using premium, ethically-sourced fabrics. We never outsource our production.

Does Peaches & Plushies take custom commissions?

Yes! Custom commissions are open. You can reach out via email at peachesnplushies@gmail.com or through our Patreon.

Do you ship outside of the United States?

If you are outside of the US email me and I will try to accommodate. I will not pay for custom fees.

Can children play with these plushies?

I do not recommend letting children play with handsewn plushies. The stitches are fragile. These are art pieces, meant to be on display and loved gently. Inside these plushies, there are beads and polyester stuffing, which can be dangerous for children.

Where do you get your patterns?

Credit can be found in the description of every post. Here are pattern makers I use: BeeZeeArt, TypingWithTea, RobynaBobbin, DelilahIris, and myself :P

How long does shipping take?

I ship First Class with USPS. Usually it will take 3-6 days, but may take longer if there is a holiday/shipping delay.

What if the plushie gets lost in the mail?

Once I drop off the package with all the info you've given me, it is out of my hands. I can send you links and info of what to do depending on the situation, but I will not give any refunds or replacements. Always double check the shipping address you use.

How to take care of your plushie!

Very gently pull the limbs and massage into a pleasing shape. Softly spot clean using a damp cloth if needed. Use small scissors to get rid of the pilling, don't go over any of the stitching!
