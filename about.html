About Peaches N' Plushies

Handcrafted with love and a little bit of magic

Our Story

My name is Teddy and I'm the fiber artist behind Peaches N' Plushies. The name is a tribute to my beloved pup, Peaches, who passed away in 2022. She was the inspiration to start this journey and her memory is stitched into every creation.

Creating has always been a hobby of mine, and I've been sewing professionally for over 4 years. I opened my own website to sell my felt creations when I was 18 in 2020. The day after my 18th birthday, I moved to Washington and pursued my sewing career full-time!

Meet the Maker

Hello! I'm Teddy, the hands and heart behind every plushie. Your support helps me keep creating and living my dream. I love being a fiber artist, and I'm so grateful for the opportunity to share my passion with you.

Every plushie is designed, stitched, and packaged by me in my small studio. It's a process fueled by coffee and a love for all things cute and cuddly.
